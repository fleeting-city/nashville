The one pattern to notice

They split into two camps and both win: photo-forward editorial (Time Out, Eater, Goldbelly - big images, magazine energy) and radical-minimal curation (The Skint - no photos, no capital letters, just Event / date / where / cost / one line). What every strong one shares: the date, price, and location sit on every single item. Fleeting's countdown chip is already that instinct - the question is just how much photography you want around it.
